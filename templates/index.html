Detailed description

This project is related to subjective image quality assessment, applied on gray-level images, made in collaboration with Computational Sostainability
Unit (CSU) of University of Pavia. There are different kinds of images, that are falling into three different categories:
- Original: the images with no degradation;
- Impaired 1: the images with a small degradation;
- Impaired 2: the images with a heavy, and more visible, degradation;
All the used images are copyright free, so you can use them to perform other experiments and modify them.
